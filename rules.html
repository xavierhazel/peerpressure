Included in the Game

Game Pieces

52 Action Cards

12 Character/Ranking Cards

1 Servo Clock Timer

Gameplay

The Game Begins

Each player picks a character card and a task card

- DO NOT SHOW TO ANYONE

All remaining task cards are put in the middle

How To Play

- Players start the 15 minutes timer and begin convincing

- Once a player completes one task, they will keep that card in their pile and pick up their next task card

- The teacher event will trigger (See Teacher section)

- After the timer is up, the players will reveal their character and calculate their points (See Roles and Point Mechanics)


Mechanics

Primary Mechanics

Within rounds, players have to convince other players to commit as many tasks as possible in 15 minutes.

Task Cards

Task cards can be broken into Action and Speaking cards.

Action Cards:You must convince another player to perform the action listed on the card. There are restrictions on what you can say/do when convincing them. All of these cards are blue.

Speaking Cards: You must convince another player to say items in the category listed on the card. There are restrictions on what you can say and on what the other player (the person you are trying to convince) can use as an answer. All of these cards are red.

Roles And Point Mechanics

Role cards determine the amount of points a player will earn from convincing that player (All points are located on the card)

Make sure to keep track of those you convinced!

- Each character is listed on your character card with a corresponding number

- That number is how many points you will earn

The player with the most amount of points wins!
