Included in the Game

Game Pieces

52 Action Cards

12 Character/Ranking Cards

1 Servo Clock Timer

Game Play

Primary Mechanics

Within rounds, players have to convince other players to commit as many tasks as possible in 15 minutes.

< Task Cards

Task cards can be broken into Action and Speaking cards.

Action Cards:You must convince another player to perform the action listed on the card. There are restrictions on what you can say/do when convincing them. All of these cards are blue.

Speaking Cards: You must convince another player to say items in the category listed on the card. There are restrictions on what you can say and on what the other player (the person you are trying to convince) can use as an answer. All of these cards are red.

Roles And Rank Mechanics

Role cards determine the acceptability and influence of other players following how their role cards interact, the characters and their roles in detention include

-Jock: Skipping too much class

-Popular girl: On her phone in class

-Cool kid: Sleeping in Class

-Nerd: helping grade

-Weird kid: Plagiarized on essay

-Teacher: supervises student

-Each role card has different influences on the others as indicated on the card. These influences affect the ranking of the players in the game Once a player convinces another player to commit an action, their ranking increases by their influence on the influenced player’s role, and the influenced player's rank decreases by half of their influence over the influencers.

Teacher

In the middle of the games 15 min period, a Sound is released from the clock, once this sound is played, the person with the teacher cards needs to identify anyone talking at the time of the event (maybe half their ranks,idk)

beginning of the game

each player picks a character card, not showing it to any other players.

Average Gameplay round action order

-All players receive their action card (never showing it to anyone)and decide on a person to try to convince -Players start the timer and begin convincing -Teacher event triggers -People do or don't do their action -Rank changes are calculated (at this point character cards are revealed) The player’s rank is equal to or exceeds 24

Victory condition

The player’s rank is equal to or exceeds 24
